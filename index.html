Project Guidelines

The app must be open source

IT requires time tracking via hackatime

It must be built during the Hack Club YSWS duration

You have to dedicate 5+ for your app. THis is the minimum requirement

The app must have 4 unique screen (e.g home, about,setting,feature-screen, etc)

You can use any language supported by latest version of Android

The App must have 1 unique features that makes your app different

FAQ

How many projects can I build?

For now, we're limiting it to one submission per person.

What is PlayVerse?

PlayVerse is a Hack Club YSWS where you'll build your own Android app from scratch .

Do I need prior experience in Android development?

No experience is required to join PlayVerse. We welcome beginners and will guide you through the entire process.

Who is eligible?

Anyone who is a high school student (or younger).

What do I need?

Any device that can run Android Studio (or you can use VS Code )

Which programming language should I use?

Whatever you want! Kotlin, React Native, Flutter -we support all of them. Just pick one and stick with it (seriously, don't switch halfway through).
